# Sample-Size Stability

This section documents that the cross-prompt agreement findings reported throughout the paper are stable across the sample sizes used for development and validation (Section~\ref{sec-results-stability}). Figure~\ref{fig:sample-size-stability} reports filtered-median $\bar{\kappa}^{\text{TriState}}$ with 95\% bootstrap confidence intervals at five sample sizes spanning two orders of magnitude, from a {{refinement_150_n}}-note refinement set to a {{extended_5000_n}}-note extended validation set. Confidence intervals overlap across sample sizes, indicating that the agreement metric has converged sufficiently that subsequent comparisons reflect substantive findings rather than sample-size artifacts.
